About Me My Portfolio

Victoria grew up in Gloucester, VA (do not get confused with Gloucester, NJ). Gloucester County is a county in the Commonwealth of Virginia. As of the 2020 census, the population was 38,711. Its county seat is Gloucester Courthouse. The county was founded in 1651 in the Virginia Colony and is named for Henry Stuart, Duke of Gloucester.

Education

After obtaining a high school diploma, Victoria attended Virginia Tech and graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Human Development. Human Development is a multidisciplinary field that encompasses the study of different stages of life, from birth to older adulthood. It takes a closer look at the processes of growth and change that occur across individuals' lifespans, in addition to the study of individuals and families from a multidisciplinary perspective.

With an interest in working with students, she attended Radford University's Counselor Education program with a concentration in School Counseling. Unfortunately, Victoria left due to unforseen circumstances affecting the program.

Without dismay, Victoria was able to register as a student for Grand Circus's C# coding bootcamp, where she is able to pursue a long-time interest.

Hobbies

Victoria enjoys spending her free time working out, dancing, loving her 2 cats, and playing games with friends. She has danced ballet since the age of 4, along with other styles of dance such as jazz, modern, character, and recently urban hip-hop.
